# Triple C Principles & Mechanics

## Core Values
- **Clean** — Physical and digital environments. Actionable improvement.
- **Connect** — Human relationships and community belonging.
- **Community** — Shared impact that is visible and measurable.

## Gamification Philosophy
Transform individual actions into visible community landmarks and shared progress. The goal is authentic engagement, not manufactured virality.

## Content & Storytelling Rules
- Prefer real CJS job outcomes as source material when possible (highest authenticity).
- Every piece of content should answer: “What changed in the community because of this action?”
- Impact scoring must be grounded in observable results (e.g., properties transformed, areas cleaned, connections made).

## Integration with CJS
CJS job completion → Automatic opportunity for Triple C content hook.  
Example: “Another Pacific Beach property restored — one more step toward a cleaner, more connected neighborhood.”
