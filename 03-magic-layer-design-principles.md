# Magic Layer Design Principles

## Primary Objective
Create high-leverage, teachable, productizable AI systems that deliver $1k–2k/hr effective value.

## Core Design Standards
- **Recursion** — Systems that improve themselves with use.
- **Modularity** — Components can be reused, remixed, or licensed.
- **Clarity** — A student or operator can understand the “why” and the “how” quickly.
- **Grounded in Reality** — Best case studies and templates come from actual CJS operations and CrewRoute builds.

## Productization Path
Every significant system built for CJS or CrewRoute OS should be evaluated for:
1. Can this be turned into a template or course module?
2. Can another operator or business use a version of this?
3. Does it demonstrate the Magic Layer methodology clearly?

## Relationship to Empire
Magic Layer is the leverage multiplier. It turns operational wins in CJS into educational assets and future revenue while sharpening the tools the rest of the empire uses.
