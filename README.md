# CrewRoute OS

**The complete operating system for serious landscaping operators.**

Built and battle-tested at CJS Landscape Solutions.

---

## What is CrewRoute OS?

CrewRoute OS is a fully automated landscaping operations system that replaces spreadsheets, manual dispatching, and guesswork with a clean, intelligent workflow.

It is designed for solo operators and small crews who want to stop being the bottleneck in their own business and start running their operation like a modern, leveraged system.

---

## Core Features

- **Waste Score Engine**  
  Real-time scoring of every property (0–100) based on route overlap, crew idle time, fuel burn, and unbilled services. Helps you instantly see where money and time are being lost.

- **Automated Morning Dispatch**  
  Runs at 6:30 AM every day. Automatically prioritizes the highest-penalty jobs first so you start the day with maximum efficiency.

- **Tribe Score System**  
  Gamifies crew performance and accountability. Turns daily operations into measurable, motivating progress instead of chaotic execution.

- **Photo-to-Estimate Intelligence**  
  Snap photos on-site and generate structured estimates quickly. Reduces time spent on manual quoting and improves consistency.

- **Operator → AI Director Shift**  
  Moves you from doing every task yourself to directing intelligent systems that handle estimating, routing, logging, and follow-up.

## Why CrewRoute OS Exists

Most landscaping businesses are limited by the owner’s time and energy. CrewRoute OS was built to remove that ceiling.

Instead of being the person who estimates, schedules, follows up, and chases payments, you become the person who **designs and directs the system** that does those things.

This is the shift from Operator to AI Director.
