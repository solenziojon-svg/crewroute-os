# Cross-Pillar Synergy Rules

## Mandatory Synergy Scan
On every significant task, ask:
- How does this CJS operation create Triple C content or proof?
- How does this build a Magic Layer case study or template?
- What real-world pattern here could inform Crypto Social Arbitrage?
- How can Magic Layer frameworks improve this CJS process?

## Data Flow Standards
- **CJS → Triple C**: Job photos, before/afters, and completion data are primary sources for authentic content.
- **CJS → Magic Layer**: Operational patterns, pricing models, agent designs, and dispatch logic become case studies and templates.
- **Magic Layer → CJS**: Frameworks, prompts, and architectural patterns are applied back into CrewRoute OS and daily operations.
- **CJS → Crypto Social Arbitrage**: Cash flow timing, pricing elasticity, and service business constraints provide ground truth.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Siloed work that only serves one pillar.
- Content that feels manufactured instead of earned from real operations.
- Building systems that cannot be taught or productized.
